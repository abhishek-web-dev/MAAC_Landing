Learning Journey

From Beginner to
Studio-Ready Artist

Phase 01 — Art Fundamentals
Drawing principles, perspective, character design, storyboarding, and the language of animation. We build your artistic foundation before touching software.
Phase 02 — 3D Modelling & Sculpting
Polygon modelling, sub-division surfaces, UV unwrapping, ZBrush sculpting, retopology, and texture baking with Substance Painter.
Phase 03 — Rigging & Animation
Skeleton setup, IK/FK systems, facial rigging, weight painting, animation principles — walk cycles, acting, lip sync, and performance animation.
Phase 04 — Simulation & FX
Cloth, hair, rigid body, fluid simulations, and Houdini particle systems. Create destruction, explosions, and natural FX with industry-grade tools.
Phase 05 — Lighting & Rendering
Physically-based lighting, HDRI setups, Arnold GPU rendering, multi-pass AOVs, and colour management in ACES for film-quality output.
Phase 06 — Showreel & Placement
Final film project, character animation reel, portfolio review, mock studio interviews, and direct access to our placement drives with 50+ hiring partners.
